About Barrow-AI

Built by athletes.
Obsessed with AI.

Barrow AI began at Georgetown University, founded by two college athletes with a shared obsession for AI and the future of search.

While studying the shift toward Artificial Intelligence, we realized that while AI is the future, many boutique businesses are technically ill-equipped to compete in it. We started small, helping local Georgetown businesses optimize their digital DNA so they wouldn't just exist in the AI era — they would lead it.

Today, Barrow AI has scaled that mission. We ensure that in an era of AI-driven discovery, your business is the one the models find first.
